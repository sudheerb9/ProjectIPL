About Us

   Sports is something that unites everyone irrespective of age, culture, language and other differences. Ashvamedha, the Sports Fest of IIT Bhubaneswar, intends to promote sports as a part of life, a competitive spirit, will power to never give up and feeling of comradery among the people. We organize an annual Inter-College Sports tournament along with engaging sports activities including but not limited to Quizzes, Inter-branch events and online events. With a team of highly motivated people we are on a never ending quest to push the boundaries and take Ashvamedha to greater heights. Working tirelessly to make Ashvamedha one of the most coveted competitions not only in Bhubaneswar but entire state of Odisha.


Rules

Logging in and participation:

  • Only individual participants will be allowed. (no team participation).
  • Every participating individual must login through his/her institute email ID. (no entries through any personal email addresses will be accepted).
  • At the start of the IPL, every participant must choose 4 teams that they think are going to qualify for the playoffs and also the team among them that is going to win.
  • Before every match, each participant must predict the playing 11, the winning team and the man of the match.

Points scheme:

  • Correctly guessing a playoff team: 25 points per correctly guessed team.
  • Correctly guessing the winner: 25 points.
  • Correctly guessing winner for each match: 10 points per match.
  • Correctly guessing the man of the match: 10 points per match.
  • Playing 11 guess: 1 point for each correctly guessed player

    • Max 'match score' possible in a match= 22+10+10 (11x2 playing 11’s + winner + MoTM)

Streak points:

If you play for continuous days, one point will be added in the streak, and the effective point for that match will be: Final match score + Streak points.
  • Streak will reduce to half of it’s value if you miss one match, and the same pattern will follow.
  • Streak points will freeze after 15 points and no further increment will be made. Keep in mind that even if your streak points are maximum (15), missing a match will reduce it to 15/2=7 and it can further again be increased to 15 by playing continuously.
    • Example: Let P denote match participated in and M denote a miss.
    If someone participated and scored 20 points (match score) every day they played and :

    P P P P P M P P P P M M P
    Points will be given as: 20 21 22 23 24 0 22 23 24 25 0 0 21
    Note: Zero for missing the match and after every M, streak points get halved.

Points table and winner:

  • This is a branch wise competition, there are 9 teams as:

    • CSE, ECE, EE, MECH, META, CIVIL, M1, M2, PHD, Alumni

  • Every day, the top 5 scorers from each team will contribute to the team’s score and those 5 names will be featured in everyday's leaderboard.
  • The team wise scoreboard will be updated every day.
  • The winning player will receive Ashvamedha merchandise and goodies.
